Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
  • 4 brioche burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Your favorite sauce (ketchup, mayo, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Gently form the ground beef into four equal patties. Make a small divot in the center of each.
  2. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill or pan-fry the patties for 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  4. In the last minute, place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover to melt.
  5. Lightly toast the brioche buns.
  6. Assemble the burger: bottom bun, sauce, patty, tomato, onion, lettuce, and top bun.
  7. Serve immediately!
